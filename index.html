Welcome


We’re the Construction Recruitment Agency Who’s Making Recruitment Better.


Since 2001, we’ve been bringing our unique brand of construction recruitment to some of the most exciting Candidates and Clients in the UK and beyond. Up and down the country, we have expert teams of Construction Recruitment Consultants and Support Staff who are dedicated to providing a better brand of temporary, contract and permanent recruitment across a range of specialist industry sectors. But what makes us different? It’s the way we think about People that sets us apart. Linear Recruitment was founded by our Managing Director, Adam Turner, to place People at the heart of recruitment. We stood by our Values ever since then, and they’re what make us who we are. Our specialist teams are based in Newcastle, Leeds, Manchester, and Sheffield and we also have a dedicated construction recruitment agency in London. They serve Construction Recruitment and Property, Rail, Energy and Power, Trades and Labour, Design and Consultancy, and Industrial and Engineering recruitment sectors.


Work For Us



We wholeheartedly believe that a successful business is a result of the People at the heart of it, and that’s why we invest so heavily in our construction recruitment Linear Team. Of course we’re professional and ambitious, but there’s something extra special about our People. It’s their honest and caring nature that sets us apart. And how do we attract and retain the best People? Put simply, we encourage and support our construction recruitment consultants to grow and develop. Our People are loyal to us because they understand we help them to achieve their goals and develop professionally and personally. In fact, many of our senior Employees are proud to have started their careers with us as juniors, working their way up to the senior positions they excel in now. Construction Employment | Linear RecruitmentWe always want to hear from People who like to do things better. It doesn’t matter if you’re looking for a brand new career or have years of experience under your belt. If you love the sound of Linear Recruitment, and relate to our Values, we’d love to see how you can fit into our team. Our goal is to promote a culture that reflects our values and engage the best people, who love to work for us by being a UK Employer of Choice.


History

Our agency is established 12 years ago by Ventsislav Kostadinov. He saw an opportunity for recruitment consultants to match talented building professionals to a role within a company where they could make a difference.
